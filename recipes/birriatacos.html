Birra Jackfruit Tacos

by Amanda Elgin

Ingredients

  • Canned Jackfruit
  • Tortillas
  • Onions
  • Adobo Chili peppers
  • Cheese

Directions

Boil jackfruit for 20 minutes. While jackfruit is boiling blend adobo peppers and tomato paste.

Mix jackfruit and blended sauce, top tortillas with birria mixture.. Top with cheese and onions and cilantro
